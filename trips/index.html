Common questions

Need help getting started? Write to connect@sparkyminis.com.

Do I need to install anything?

No installs required. LipiKit Trips runs inside any modern browser. Add it to your home screen for a native app feel.

Can I work offline?

Absolutely. After your first visit, the app caches everything locally so you can plan on flights or in remote destinations.

How does sync work?

Sync is optional. Share a secure PIN with co-travelers to collaborate. You can disable sync anytime and data remains on your device.

How do I support LipiKit?
